Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 27, 2026

SparkyPawsCare.com ("we," "us," "our") is operated by Spark Paws. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have about it. We aim to be plain-spoken: if something here is unclear, email us at privacy@sparkypawscare.com.

1. Information we collect

We collect only the information necessary to operate our newsletter and improve our content:

  • Email address. Collected when you opt in to our newsletter via sparkpaws.com, sparkypawscare.com, or a verified partner.
  • Name or first name (optional). Used to personalize emails if you provide it.
  • Engagement data. Whether emails were delivered, opened, or clicked. Used to keep our list clean and our content useful.
  • Basic site analytics. When you visit sparkypawscare.com, we may collect anonymized data like page views, referrer, browser type, and country. We do not use cookies for advertising or cross-site tracking.

2. How we use your information

We use your information to:

  • Send you the SparkyPawsCare newsletter you opted in to receive.
  • Operate, maintain, and improve the website and the newsletter.
  • Process unsubscribe requests, bounces, and complaints in real time.
  • Comply with legal obligations under the EU GDPR, the Irish Data Protection Act 2018, the ePrivacy Regulations, and equivalent laws in jurisdictions where our subscribers reside (including the UK GDPR, the US CAN-SPAM Act, and the California CCPA/CPRA).

3. What we do not do

We do not:

  • Sell your personal information. Ever. To anyone. For any reason.
  • Share your email with third parties for their own marketing.
  • Buy email lists or scrape addresses from the web.
  • Send to anyone who didn't opt in. Every recipient is on our list because they took an explicit action to join.
  • Use cross-site advertising cookies or behavioral retargeting pixels in our emails.

4. Service providers

We use a small set of service providers strictly to deliver our newsletter and operate this website. Each is bound by a written agreement that limits their use of your information to providing services to us:

  • Listmonk (self-hosted on our own infrastructure) — manages our subscriber list and sends emails.
  • Amazon Web Services (AWS SES) — relays our outgoing emails to recipient mail servers.
  • Our hosting provider — serves sparkypawscare.com.

5. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Unsubscribe at any time via the link in any email, our unsubscribe page, or by emailing unsubscribe@sparkypawscare.com.
  • Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request deletion of your personal information from our systems.
  • Object to processing or request restriction of processing of your data.

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@sparkypawscare.com. We will respond within 30 days.

6. Data retention

We retain your email address only as long as you remain subscribed. When you unsubscribe, your email is moved to a suppression list (so we never accidentally email you again) and the rest of your personal data is deleted within 90 days unless we are legally required to retain it.

7. Children's privacy

SparkyPawsCare is intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 16. If you believe a child has subscribed, contact us and we will remove them.

8. Where we operate and international transfers

SparkyPawsCare is operated by Spark Paws from Ireland. As an Ireland-based operator, we process personal data in accordance with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the Irish Data Protection Act 2018, and the ePrivacy Regulations 2011 (S.I. 336/2011). The legal bases for our processing are: consent (you opted in to receive the newsletter) and legitimate interests (operating, securing, and improving the Service).

Some of our service providers (for example, Amazon Web Services) may process data in jurisdictions outside the European Economic Area. Where this occurs, transfers are made under the European Commission's Standard Contractual Clauses or another GDPR-recognised transfer mechanism.

If you are a resident of the EU/EEA or the United Kingdom, you have full GDPR/UK GDPR rights as described in Section 5. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Irish Data Protection Commission (dataprotection.ie) or your local supervisory authority. If you are a resident of California, you have additional rights under the CCPA/CPRA — email privacy@sparkypawscare.com to exercise them.

9. Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and notify subscribers via email when appropriate.

10. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be sent to privacy@sparkypawscare.com or to the mailing address on our contact page.
