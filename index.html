Who we are

The pet care arm of a brand 275,000+ pet parents already trust.

SparkyPawsCare.com is the dedicated email and content hub for Spark Paws — an established pet apparel brand trusted by over 275,000 verified buyers. This site powers our daily pet care newsletter, delivering helpful, plain-text tips on dog health, nutrition, training, seasonal safety, and more to our opted-in customer list.

We never spam. We never send to purchased lists. Every subscriber is a real Spark Paws customer who opted in through our website at checkout or via an explicit signup form. Unsubscribes are processed automatically and in real time by our self-hosted email platform (Listmonk).

Want to unsubscribe?

We respect your inbox. If you no longer want to receive emails from SparkyPawsCare, you can unsubscribe at any time. Here are three ways:

  1. Click the unsubscribe link at the bottom of any email you've received from us. It's a one-click process — no hoops, no guilt trips.
  2. Email us directly at unsubscribe@sparkypawscare.com with the subject line "Unsubscribe" and we'll remove you within 24 hours.
  3. Use our preference center to manage subscription settings, including content frequency. Go to the preference center →

Our email platform (Listmonk, self-hosted) processes all unsubscribe requests, hard bounces, and complaints automatically and in real time. Once you unsubscribe, you won't hear from us again unless you re-subscribe yourself.
