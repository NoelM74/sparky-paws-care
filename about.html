About

The editorial home of Spark Paws.

SparkPawsCare exists for one reason: to give pet parents who already trust Spark Paws something genuinely useful in their inbox every morning.

Who we are

SparkPawsCare.com is operated by Spark Paws, a premium pet apparel and accessories brand that has served over 275,000 verified customers worldwide. Spark Paws designs and sells dog clothing, harnesses, walking gear, shoes, owner-matching sets, and other thoughtfully-made products that pet parents love.

This site is the home of our daily pet care newsletter and a growing library of evidence-informed pet care content. We don't run a separate publishing business — we make pet products, and these emails are how we stay in touch with the people who buy them.

Why we send a newsletter

Most brand newsletters are sales pitches dressed up as helpful content. Ours is the inverse: useful content with the brand quietly behind it. If we earn enough of your trust through good information, the products will sell themselves. That's the whole strategy.

Every email is one short, plain-text tip — typically 90 seconds to read. There are no images. There are no banner ads. Roughly once a month, we'll mention a Spark Paws product when it's genuinely relevant to the topic. That's it.

Who writes the content

SparkPawsCare content is researched and written by an in-house editorial team that draws on peer-reviewed veterinary literature, established animal behavior research, and consultation with practicing veterinarians. We are not veterinarians and our content is not a substitute for veterinary care — if your pet shows signs of illness, please contact your vet.

We cite specific studies and named institutions (the Family Dog Project in Budapest, the AVMA, the AAHA, etc.) wherever the article is making a research-based claim. When we're sharing our opinion, we say so.

How you got on our list

Every subscriber to our newsletter is on the list because they took an explicit action to join, in one of these ways:

  • Opted in at checkout on sparkpaws.com
  • Submitted an opt-in form on sparkpaws.com or sparkypawscare.com
  • Signed up through a verified partner integration

We do not buy lists. We do not scrape email addresses. We do not share or sell your email address. Ever. See our Privacy Policy for the full picture.

How email compliance works on our end

Our newsletter is sent through Listmonk, an open-source, self-hosted email platform. Every message includes a one-click unsubscribe link. Unsubscribes, hard bounces, and complaints are processed automatically and in real time. Once you unsubscribe, you stop receiving emails immediately — there is no manual review queue, no waiting period.

We also honor List-Unsubscribe headers, supporting Gmail and Yahoo's one-click unsubscribe button in their email clients.

Contact

For anything email-related, write to unsubscribe@sparkypawscare.com. For anything product-related, please use the contact channels at sparkpaws.com. For general inquiries about SparkPawsCare itself, see our contact page.
