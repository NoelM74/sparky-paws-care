Unsubscribe

We respect your inbox.

No hoops. No guilt trips. Here are three ways to stop receiving emails from SparkyPawsCare — pick whichever is easiest.

Three ways to unsubscribe

  1. One-click from any email. Open any email you've received from us and click the unsubscribe link at the bottom. It's a single click — no form, no login, no confirmation step. You're done.
  2. Email us directly. Send an email to unsubscribe@sparkypawscare.com with the subject line "Unsubscribe". We'll remove you within 24 hours.
  3. Use the preference center. If you'd like to manage your preferences (frequency, content type) instead of unsubscribing entirely, our Listmonk preference center lets you do that. The link is in every email we send.

How we process unsubscribes

Our email platform — Listmonk, an open-source, self-hosted system — processes all unsubscribe requests, hard bounces, and complaints automatically and in real time. There is no manual review queue. There is no waiting period.

Once you unsubscribe, you will not receive another email from us unless you re-subscribe yourself by opting in again at sparkpaws.com.

We also honor the List-Unsubscribe headers required by Gmail and Yahoo, so the one-click unsubscribe button inside your email client will work as well.

Reporting spam or abuse

If you believe you are receiving emails from us that you did not opt in to, or if you suspect abuse of any kind, please contact us at abuse@sparkypawscare.com. We investigate every report and take list hygiene extremely seriously.

We do not buy email lists. We do not scrape. We do not engage in any practice that would put a non-consenting recipient on our list. If you ended up subscribed without intent, we want to know — both so we can remove you and so we can investigate how it happened.
